How it started

Mr. Ramesh Kumar, Mechanical Draughtsmen having 28 years of rich experience in product development, large scale production and industrial management & always strived towards customer satisfaction by providing quality product and on time delivery always.

Sai Precision Industries was started by Ramesh Kumar B.N in head office in Silicon City Bangalore in the year 2013 with a vision to make his firm known for its “Quality and Deliverable”.

Mr. Shashank B R, an Engineering Graduate having 4 years of experience in software and production line, quality control, engineering applications joined Sai Precision Industries as Partner to support other functions of the organization which includes Commercial activities, Documentation, Statutory obligations, Product development, & continual improvement of the organization.

Sai Precision Industries after due & stringent process and evaluation registered as Channel Partners for Ashirvad Pipes Private Limited in the year 2013 for supply of precision bore well accessories.

With the support from Dedicated, Experienced team we are able to achieve the Quality & timely deliverables as per client expectations always. We believe in continual improvement programmes and all the team members are provided periodical on the job trainings by subject experts to align every individual in achieving organization goals & growth of our organization.
